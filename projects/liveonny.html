LiveOnNY is a nonprofit organization committed to helping New York live on through organ and tissue donation. They provide support and comfort to donors and their loved ones throughout the donation process, and for years after, while also educating and inspiring communities through educational events, media outreach, and local work.

We were sub-contracted to bring to life the LiveOnNY rebrand project. We provided creative direction, full web and mobile UX / UI, and graphic design work.

Creative Direction

One of the main objectives with this rebrand was to update the LiveOnNY creative direction with a new focus. We strategically shifted the narrative away from traditionally dry statistics to relationships in a micro-level. We felt it was important to focus on compassion and empathy. We felt it was important to focus on people.

In total, we created 3 concept packages and worked with the clients to ultimately select the one that best represented their values and mission statement.

Concept

It’s not just about saving a life. It’s about saving a Father / Mother / Son / Daughter / Grandparent / Grandchild / Uncle / Aunt.

Thinking about the act in itself is only half of the story. It’s also about what happens after; the results and the lives affected by those results - not just those receiving or giving a transplant, but about the lives these individuals indirectly (and many times directly) impact.

Color & Typography

We were given the directive to maintain some of the original identity from the branding and rather than completely dismantling what came before, we opted to instead elevate it further. Font styles were updated to something more modern and accessible.

Key colors were carried over with the addition of a monotone blue as a base in order to create more consistency between different sections on the site and to offset harsh contrasts.

Graphics & Iconography

Bold and direct content will be balanced with a light delivery to omit any imposition. Graphics will communicate a sense of familiarity and welcome and icons will be non-intrusive. It’s ok to be here and it’s ok to feel whatever you’re feeling.

Final Site Design

Once exploration concluded, we combined new and existing elements to bring the design language to life across the new site. We redesigned all interior pages in addition to the homepage, as well as delivering a completely revamped mobile experience.
