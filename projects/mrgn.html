MRGN is a business intelligence platform for small businesses, serving as a crystal ball into their finances and operations and helping them mitigate future risks by leveraging big data and Machine learning.

We began with logo design and creating different user journeys to map out the full MVP experience. We then worked on product design which included: initial wireframes, finalized high-fidelity UI, a fully interactive clickable prototype, and various iterations of financial modeling features.
